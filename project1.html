Fitness AI Agent

GitHub

The Problem

With the growing interest in personalized fitness, individuals often struggle to create tailored workout and nutrition plans based on their unique requirements. Generic fitness advice lacks the ability to adapt dynamically to user-specific needs, leading to inefficient workouts and poor results. Furthermore, tracking progress and understanding complex fitness metrics often requires expertise, which many users lack.

Solution

The Fitness AI Agent is a multi-agent workout application that leverages LangFlow and GPT-3.5 Turbo to provide personalized macro and calorie recommendations for users. Using advanced AI workflows, the app dynamically adapts workout plans and offers real-time fitness insights.

Key features include:

  • Real-time macro and calorie calculations based on user profiles
  • Dynamic adjustment of workout plans using AI-driven insights
  • Integration of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for accurate fitness queries
  • Storage of user data in a vector database to enhance future interactions
  • Support for complex fitness queries, such as weight-lifting calculations and progress tracking

Target Users

  • Fitness enthusiasts looking for tailored workout and nutrition plans
  • Beginners seeking simple yet effective fitness guidance
  • Personal trainers and coaches aiming to improve client engagement
  • Individuals with specific fitness goals, such as weight loss or muscle gain
